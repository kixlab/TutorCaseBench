SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is participating in a 4-square handball game during the lunch break and needs to wait their turn to get into a square.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for our lunch break 4-square handball game. There is only one game going, so you will need to wait your turn to get into a square.

STUDENT: There are nine of us waiting. I don't want to wait, I want to get into a square right now.

TUTOR: You just have to wait in the line.

STUDENT: (Waits, gets into the square, and then misses the ball) I wasn't out! The ball bounced on the line!

TUTOR: You are out. Please step out of the square and go to the back of the line.

STUDENT: (Throws a temper tantrum) No! I'm not leaving the square! You have to let me keep playing!
