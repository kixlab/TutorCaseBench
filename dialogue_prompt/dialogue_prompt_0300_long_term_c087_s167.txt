DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for independent work. Please start working quietly on your assigned tasks.

STUDENT: (Turns to a peer) Hey, did you see the game last night?

TUTOR: Craig, remember the rules for independent work time.

STUDENT: I am just asking a question. (Turns back to peer) Seriously though, what did you think of that last play?

TUTOR: Please focus on your own assignment.

STUDENT: (Laughs as peer responds back) I know, right? It was crazy! Look at this drawing I did of it.
