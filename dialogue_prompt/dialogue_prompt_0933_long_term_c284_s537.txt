DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's begin our 20-minute discussion on the 'do now' question. How did the U.S. change after September 11? You can look at the responses we put on the front board.

STUDENT: Privacy is being taken away. The country has become more strict.

TUTOR: Can you say a bit more about that?

STUDENT: The government is more secretive. I just do not trust the government. Look what happened to Arab Americans after 9/11.

TUTOR: What do the rest of you think about this?

STUDENT: We all agree. We are all equal and they should not be discriminated against because they are Japanese Americans or Arab Americans. This is just an act of racism.
