SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

José is completing a pre-TAKS math drill worksheet in Ms. Kendall's fifth-grade classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Alright everyone, please complete the pre-TAKS math drill worksheet on your desks.

STUDENT: (Scribbles answers rapidly down the page without reading the questions) Done! Hey Manuel, did you see how fast I finished?

TUTOR: José, please check your work and stay quiet.

STUDENT: I did check it. See? Behave good. Do my work with a great smile. (Grins exaggeratedly at the class and throws a crumpled piece of paper at another student's desk)

TUTOR: You need to sit in your seat and wait for the others to finish the math drill.

STUDENT: (Stands up and walks over to another desk) These drills are boring anyway. Hey, who's going to the Spaghetti Warehouse later? Watch this! (Starts balancing a pencil on his nose and making loud jokes to get the class laughing)
