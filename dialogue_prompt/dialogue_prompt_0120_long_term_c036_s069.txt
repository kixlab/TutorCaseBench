SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Gail is taking a Science test in her classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Gail, please put your extra credit poster board away and start your Science test.

STUDENT: (Takes an inordinate amount of time to put her poster board along the wall, then sits down and takes a long time choosing a different pen while watching the girl beside her put her hair in a ponytail holder)

TUTOR: Please focus on your paper and keep working.

STUDENT: (Gets up to use the restroom, spends two minutes rearranging the keys on the hooks, and takes a circuitous route back to her desk after signing back in)

TUTOR: There are 15 minutes remaining in the Science period. Please finish your test.

STUDENT: (Starts writing on her hand, marks one answer on her test, then goes across the room to wash the stuff off her hands at the sink. She comes back and begins to draw on her knee, below her skirt)
