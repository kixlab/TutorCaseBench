──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is supposed to be sitting down to listen to instructions for an English communicative activity in a large, hot classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Everyone, please settle down and take your seats so we can listen to the instructions for our English communicative activity.

STUDENT: (Standing by the window instead of sitting) I don't want to do this right now. The ceiling fans don't work and it was very hot in here.

TUTOR: We need to practice expressing our ideas in English today. Please go to your seat.

STUDENT: I don't understand how to say anything in English anyway. I'm not participating.

TUTOR: Just try your best to listen to the instructions first.

STUDENT: (Yelling across the room to another student) I'm not doing it, viejo! You can't make us sit down and listen in this heat!
