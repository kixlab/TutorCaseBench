DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Let's look at your academic task checklist and use the letter mnemonic to study the vocabulary words for the key characteristics of matter.

STUDENT: I don't want to read these words. The time I spent out of class has left me too far behind.

TUTOR: Keep trying to sound it out so we can finish the checklist.

STUDENT: (Pushes the academic task checklist off the desk and shouts) I said I'm not reading it! I'm not doing this stupid science work anymore!
