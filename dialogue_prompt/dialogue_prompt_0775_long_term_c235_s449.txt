SCENARIO

Martin is participating in a support group discussion to devise a group pledge with other pupils.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's start our group discussion to come up with ideas for our support group pledge.

STUDENT: I was a bit shocked when I got told to come here. Didn't know what it was for a start. Wasn't too sure about it.

TUTOR: Just go ahead and share your thoughts with the group.

STUDENT: Okay, well I think the pledge should be about playing games. And we also need a rule about snacks. And I have another idea about...

TUTOR: Let's hear what the others think.

STUDENT: (Interrupting another pupil) Wait, I'm still talking! I don't think other people are entitled to make a contribution until I'm completely done. I'm just worried about getting into trouble and being branded as a trouble-maker if we mess this up!
