SCENARIO

Javier is at the board in front of the class attempting to solve a math problem to show his work after a quiz.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: I have collected the quizzes. Now you got to show your work. Javier, come to the board to solve this problem.

STUDENT: (Walks to the board in front of the class) Okay, I will write out my steps for this equation.

TUTOR: Go ahead and solve it.

STUDENT: (Attempts the problem on the board) So if I multiply these, the final answer is 24.

TUTOR: That is the wrong answer. You need to do it like you are in kindergarten, that way you make no mistakes, right? Write this 17 times. (Writes "17 times" on the board) Right, Javier?

STUDENT: (Looks at the board expressionless and falls completely silent, disengaging from the task as an act of protest.)
