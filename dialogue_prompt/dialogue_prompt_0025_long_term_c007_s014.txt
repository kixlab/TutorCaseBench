──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Ann is presented with a typical academic worksheet chosen by her teacher in the self-contained classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Ann, here is the academic worksheet I chose for you to complete today. Please start working on it.

STUDENT: (Does not look at the paper or start the task for 5 seconds) I don't want this one! Give me a different one!

TUTOR: This is the task we are doing right now. Please begin.

STUDENT: (Stands up, leaving contact with her designated chair for more than 3 seconds) No! You can't make me do it!

TUTOR: You need to sit back down in your chair and finish your work.

STUDENT: (Throws the worksheet at the teacher and runs toward the classroom door) I'm leaving!
