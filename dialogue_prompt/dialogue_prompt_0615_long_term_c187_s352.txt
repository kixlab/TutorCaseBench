TUTOR: We are going to practice how to converse in a different and appropriate manner depending on the situation. Let's start by showing me how you would ask a friend for a pencil, and then how you would ask the principal.

STUDENT: I'm already really good at this. I would just say the exact same thing to both of them: 'Hey, give me a pencil right now.'

TUTOR: Try to use code-switching and change your words for the principal.

STUDENT: (Yelling loudly) No, I'm not doing it! I don't get why you're making a weird face at me! My tone is fine and I'm not changing how I talk just because it's the principal!
