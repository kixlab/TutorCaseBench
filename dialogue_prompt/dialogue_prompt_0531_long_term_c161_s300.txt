SCENARIO

The student is sitting alone in a mainstream mathematics class, tasked with copying trigonometry formulas from the whiteboard.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please sit down at your desk and copy the trigonometry formulas from the whiteboard.

STUDENT: I feel bored all the time sitting down. Why do we have to copy all the time from the whiteboard and gaze at it?

TUTOR: Just focus on the board and write down what you see.

STUDENT: Mentally I cannot understand this. I can't just sit here and be like a statue.

TUTOR: You need to work alone and finish copying the notes.

STUDENT: I prefer standing up and working in groups rather than alone! (Stands up abruptly and starts fighting with the student at the next desk)
