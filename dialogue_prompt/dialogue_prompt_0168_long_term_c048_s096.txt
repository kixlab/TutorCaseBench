The student is arriving at a small group vocational activity to participate in a work experience project.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We are starting our vocational work experience task now. Please join the small group.

STUDENT: (Arrives looking upset) I only woke up because someone phoned me this morning to remind me. I don't want to be here.

TUTOR: Well, you are here now. Go sit with the other people on the course.

STUDENT: I'm not sitting with them. I never used to speak to anyone in school, and I don't want to talk to them right now.

TUTOR: You need to participate in the group activity to finish the task.

STUDENT: You're treating me more of a child! I'm not doing it. (Turns away, sits apart from the group, and refuses to speak)
