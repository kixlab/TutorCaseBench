SCENARIO

Juan is sitting in the 5th-grade classroom using paper and pencil to write a summary of a recently read text.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please use your paper and pencil to write a summary of the text we just read.

STUDENT: (Scribbles very quickly for a few moments and pushes the paper forward) I'm done.

TUTOR: You only wrote a few words. Can you add more details about what happened?

STUDENT: No, I'm a poor writer.

TUTOR: Why do you say that? Just keep trying to write.

STUDENT: (Erasing aggressively) Because my penmanship is bad! I keep spelling things wrong and the letters are messy. If it's not neat, it's not good writing, so I don't want to do it.
