James is sitting on the carpet with three other students playing a matching card game with compound words.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We are going to play a matching card game with these compound words on the carpet. Let's start taking turns.

STUDENT: (Jumps onto his knees and grabs the cards 'side' and 'walk') I found a match!

TUTOR: Yes, they go together, but it is not your turn. You have to return the cards to the floor.

STUDENT: (Returns the cards to the floor)

TUTOR: Okay, let's keep going with the group.

STUDENT: (Rolls onto his back, crossing his arms, and ignores the other students)
