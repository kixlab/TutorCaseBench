DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Alright everyone, you may continue to work on your film. Go ahead and organise yourselves into groups and delegate the tasks.

STUDENT: (As the other children cheer and begin to organise themselves, Adam walks away, sits by the windowsill, and takes pleasure in reading a book.)

TUTOR: Adam, shouldn't you participate in the group work?

TUTOR: You still need to do the task with your group.

STUDENT: I will go to the group as soon as they quiet down. (Continues reading his book at the windowsill)
