SCENARIO

Darin and a friend are working together during center time to build a tower using different sets of blocks.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Darin, it is center time. You and your friend need to build a tower together. Here are some square blocks for you, and some triangle blocks for your friend.

STUDENT: (Pulls all the square blocks toward himself and does not share)

TUTOR: Remember to work together to accomplish your common goal.

STUDENT: (Reaches over and takes a triangle block from his friend's pile) Give me that.

TUTOR: Your friend is asking to use that block. What should you do?

STUDENT: (Hides the block behind his back) That’s mine! I had that first.
