DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Shen, it is time to complete the assigned work for today.

STUDENT: (Remains quiet and looks around to follow the other students' actions, taking out his materials but not starting the actual assignment.)

STUDENT: (Becomes antsy in his seat, shifting around without making any progress on the assigned work.)

STUDENT: (Gets up out of his seat and begins to walk around the classroom without permission.) I don’t understand, so I won’t do it.
