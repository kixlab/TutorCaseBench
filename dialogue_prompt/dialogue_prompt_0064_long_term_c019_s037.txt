DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's look at the bar graphs in Figures 1 and 2 that show common study approaches by grade. Please compare your habits to the data.

STUDENT: I'm looking at the graphs of the common approaches by grade. I got a lower grade, so I'm comparing my habits to the approaches of those earning A's.

TUTOR: What do you notice when you look at the data?

STUDENT: Well, I study alone primarily, and I definitely take breaks while studying. I lean on breaks a lot.

TUTOR: What else stands out to you about your preparation?

STUDENT: I'm just really disappointed with my exam result. I usually study the day of or the night before my exams, and I don't really pay attention to getting sleep. I guess I just need to cram even more right before the next one.
