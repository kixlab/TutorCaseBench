──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: You have completed your first draft. Now we need to work on revising it so you have a piece of writing to take back to your general education class.

TUTOR: I have some suggestions for how you might revise it.

STUDENT: Why do I have to write it all again?

TUTOR: It is necessary to complete the task of drafting and revision.

STUDENT: (Rolls her eyes and raises her voice) I already finished! (Walks out of the classroom)
