DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Jasmine, it is time to color the objects on the page in groups of 4 on your math worksheet.

STUDENT: (Looks at Jeff) Let’s do some colors! What color is the apple? What color is the boy? What color is the car? (Excitedly shakes her braids and rubs her fingers together quickly in front of the ABC book)

STUDENT: (Grabs the ABC book again) No! Aaahh!
