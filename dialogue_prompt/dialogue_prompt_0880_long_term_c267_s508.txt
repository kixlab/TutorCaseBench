SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student and a peer are playing with dramatic play toys and cars on a carpeted floor during a free play session.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for free play. You and your partner can play with the dramatic play toys and cars on the carpet.

STUDENT: (Sits on the carpet, appearing lonely and making no comments to the peer.)

TUTOR: What are you going to play?

STUDENT: (Looks at the peer) Can I have the car?

TUTOR: Keep playing together.

STUDENT: (Does not engage in play with the peer) Can I have the car? Can I have the car? Can I have the car?
