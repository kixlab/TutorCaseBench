Joshua is participating in a small group building activity where he is expected to share blocks with his peers.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for our small group activity. You and your classmates need to share these building blocks to make a tower together.

STUDENT: (Speaking loudly) I am making the tower! I need all the big blocks!

TUTOR: Joshua, you are speaking too loudly right now.

STUDENT: No! I am not! Give me those blocks right now!

TUTOR: You need to play nicely and share the materials with the group.

STUDENT: Give me the blocks or I will hurt you!
