The student is asked to tidy up their toys during the transition from free play in the kindergarten classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Free play is over. It is time to tidy up the toys.

STUDENT: No! I am not done playing.

TUTOR: You need to put the toys in the box now.

STUDENT: (Gets angry) I said no! (Snatches a toy from the child sitting next to him)

TUTOR: Please give the toy back and start cleaning up.

STUDENT: (Throws a tantrum) No! (Kicks the toy box over and pushes the other child)
