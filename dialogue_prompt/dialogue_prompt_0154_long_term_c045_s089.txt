TUTOR: Please take out your map, notebook, and a sharpened pencil to begin the mapping assignment.

STUDENT: See? My notebook is out, too. Am I doing a good job on my map?

STUDENT: (Gets out of the seat and stands up, moving the chair) I'm not doing this! (Starts a nonacademic conversation with a peer across the room and makes inappropriate noises)
