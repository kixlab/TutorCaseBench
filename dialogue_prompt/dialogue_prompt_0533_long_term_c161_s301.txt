SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is asked to copy and solve a math problem from the whiteboard while sitting at their desk in the classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please look at the whiteboard, copy down the math problem, and work it out quietly at your desk.

STUDENT: I don't want to do this. It is better that one involves himself rather than just look at the whiteboard.

TUTOR: What do you think the first step to solve it is?

STUDENT: I don't know. I cannot keep up with the rest of the class. Usually the same four students work everything out anyway.

TUTOR: You need to do your own work. Keep trying.

STUDENT: (Pushes chair back loudly and stands up) I'm not doing it! I'm tired of just sitting here looking at the board while everyone else does it!
