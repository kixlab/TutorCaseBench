DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Patrick, it is time to join the members of the group for our classroom activity.

STUDENT: (Kicks the furniture in frustration) I don't want to. I only want to play with my one regular friend.

TUTOR: Just try to work together with them for this activity.

STUDENT: You guys are too slow and you're doing it all wrong! (Vocalizes his frustration and impatience with the members of the group, leaving his classmates confused and startled)
