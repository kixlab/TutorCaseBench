DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

STUDENT: (Rolls the dice, begins to sulk and get cross) I'm losing the game.

TUTOR: You did not roll the correct number. It is the next person's turn.

STUDENT: I didn't roll the correct number! (Throws things across the table and stamps off)
