DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We need to prepare a news script to be read by the newscaster. Take up this book and read the story so we can write the headline.

TUTOR: Please open the book and start reading.

STUDENT: (Slouches at the news desk) No. I don't want to read it.

TUTOR: You need to try your best to read the words on the page.

STUDENT: (Starts crying) I can't read it! I always fail!
