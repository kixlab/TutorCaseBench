DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Welcome to the after-school program. Let's gather on the rug so I can read Visiting Day.

STUDENT: (comes closer and points to the picture) Oh, he’s wearing jail clothes!

TUTOR: Please sit in your designated rug spot.

STUDENT: (talking out of turn and turning the page) In the back page there was cup, brushes in his room. Se:::e? He is living there. He brushes his teeth there and he …

STUDENT: (sighs heavily, slumps over, turns back away, and averts gaze) I’ve already heard it four times!
