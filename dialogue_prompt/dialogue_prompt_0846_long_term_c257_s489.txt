DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please read this grade-level passage and answer the comprehension questions at the end.

STUDENT: I want to advance in reading, so I want to make sure my answers are accurate. But as I read this first part, I asked myself, do I understand what the information is saying? I don't think I do. Can you help me?

TUTOR: Just do what you can.

STUDENT: It's difficult to control my attention in the classroom. If I just do what I can, I'm just going to write down anything to get the task completed instead of trying to understand it.

TUTOR: Keep trying to read the next paragraph.

STUDENT: I need you to help me again. I find everything... confusing. Can you just tell me what the right answers are so I can be done?
