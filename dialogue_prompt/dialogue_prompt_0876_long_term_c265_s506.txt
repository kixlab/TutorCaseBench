Martin is participating in a small-group reading activity with his peers and is expected to read his assigned materials quietly.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's start our small-group reading time. Please open your assigned materials and read the first page quietly.

STUDENT: (Turns his body completely away from the assigned reading materials)

TUTOR: Martin, you need to start reading.

STUDENT: (Gets out of his seat and leans over to the peer next to him) Hey, what are you doing after school?

TUTOR: Please sit down and focus on your work.

STUDENT: (Stays out of his seat, ignoring the assigned materials, and continues talking to his peer) I don't want to do this reading right now.
