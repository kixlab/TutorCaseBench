DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Look at this picture of the family with the toys. Tell me what is happening here.

STUDENT: Papa.

TUTOR: Okay, what else?

STUDENT: Ja. (Uses a deictic gesture to point at another toy in the picture)

TUTOR: Can you tell me more about that?

STUDENT: Nee. (Makes an iconic hand movement without speech, relying on a one-word sentence because they cannot verbally express the complex concept they have in mind)
