DIALOGUE

TUTOR: That is a lot of toys. Those trucks and movers will move out our things in the next few weeks.

STUDENT: Will we have the same teachers?

TUTOR: We will have to wait and see.

STUDENT: But will the new school have a backyard? What is going to happen to our playground after we move?
