The tutor presents a new three-week math and science work plan to the student in the classroom.

DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Here is the new math and science work plan for the next three weeks. You can go ahead and choose which tasks to begin with.

STUDENT: We use work plans, so it is not, like, do task 1, 2, 3 till tomorrow. We do what we want when we want.

TUTOR: Yes, you manage your own pace. What do you think you will work on first?

STUDENT: This is how I normally work: At school, the first couple of weeks, I do nothing, but sometimes I manage to complete a couple of tasks every lesson.

TUTOR: You should try to get started on something today.

STUDENT: (Puts the work plan aside) I find it hard to plan and organize my work. I struggle to get started, so I'll just delay it until the last few days.
