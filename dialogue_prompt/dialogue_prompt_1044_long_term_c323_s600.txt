DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please take out your science binder, turn to the lab section, complete the three questions on the board, and then bring your paper to my desk.

TUTOR: Just look through your papers and find it.

STUDENT: (starts flapping hands) It's too many steps! I can't remember what to do after I find the paper. I don't know how to start this!
