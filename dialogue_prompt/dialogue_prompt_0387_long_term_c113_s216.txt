Nancy is sitting in an individual interview during the first term to complete her 'Go for Goal!' self-evaluation report.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's begin our individual interview for the first term and fill out your 'Go for Goal!' self-evaluation report.

STUDENT: (Looks down at the desk) I don't want to do this self-evaluation right now.

TUTOR: Are there any difficulties that you might encounter?

STUDENT: I need to work hard, typing, rest... feeling nervous...

TUTOR: Just try your best to answer the questions on the report.

STUDENT: (Pushes the paper away and refuses to speak further) I can't do this interview. I don't know how to evaluate myself.
