SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is reading an 891-word social studies text, copying notes from PowerPoint slides, and answering textbook questions before a class deadline.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We have a deadline coming up. Please read this 891-word social studies text by yourself, copy the notes from the PowerPoint slides, and then answer the textbook questions.

STUDENT: I will try to answer the questions first, and if I cannot answer them, I will look back in the text. I have to economize on my resources, so I am only going to make notes on the most important information.

TUTOR: Okay, just keep working on it and make sure you read the text.

STUDENT: I feel that I don’t learn very well from merely reading a text by myself. If I am not interested in it, then it will take incredibly long to learn it.

TUTOR: Try your best to get it done before the time is up.

STUDENT: I am getting really stressed working toward this deadline. I am being slowed by having to cope with the difficult words in the text, and it takes too long for me to write down what you presented on the slides.
