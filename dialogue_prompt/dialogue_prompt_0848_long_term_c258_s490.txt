SCENARIO

The student is taking a grammar quiz during a 2nd grade whole-class language arts lesson.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Here is the grammar quiz. Please read the straightforward questions and write your answers.

STUDENT: I want to get a really high grade on this quiz to demonstrate my ability to you and to my peers.

TUTOR: Okay, go ahead and start working.

STUDENT: (Looks around the classroom) Are the other kids already on the next section? I want to make sure I'm doing better than them.

TUTOR: Keep trying to answer the questions.

STUDENT: I can't pay attention to my own questions. I keep watching everyone else because I need to see if they are finishing before me.
