SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student and tutor are reviewing a creative art work project they have been working on for a long time in the psychiatric department.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We have been working on this creative art work through a long process. Let's continue working on it today.

STUDENT: I don't want to. My willingness to cooperate and my motivation levels rise and fall, and today I just don't want to do it.

TUTOR: What do you think you should do next?

STUDENT: Nothing. Being hospitalized in a psychiatric department creates an uneasy feeling of being non-normative. This art doesn't change that.

TUTOR: Just try to look at the art work on the table.

STUDENT: (Grabs the art work) I'm in a bad mood today. I'm throwing it away!
