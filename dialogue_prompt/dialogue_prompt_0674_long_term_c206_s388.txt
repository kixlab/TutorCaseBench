The tutor and student are sitting together in the psychiatric department to prepare for an upcoming Matriculation Exam.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We need to continue preparing for your Matriculation Exam today. Let's open the study program and look at the first practice section.

STUDENT: I don't want to look at the practice test. It's too hard to function as a student right now.

TUTOR: Why don't you just try to read the first question?

STUDENT: I can't. I'm just not emotionally available for learning today. Everything feels wrong.

TUTOR: We have put a lot of effort into preparing for this exam. Please keep trying.

STUDENT: I don't care about the effort! I'm in a really bad mood today and I've decided I'm not going to take the Matriculation Exam at all.
