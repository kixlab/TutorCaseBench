SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Zeke is in the classroom preparing for recess and needs to put on his coat.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Zeke, it is time for recess. Please get your coat from the hook and put it on.

STUDENT: (Zeke takes his coat off the hook and tries to push his arm into the sleeve, but it gets stuck inside out.)

TUTOR: Keep trying, you can figure it out.

STUDENT: (Zeke pulls at the tangled sleeve and begins to whine and cry softly as he perceives the task is too difficult.)

TUTOR: Just push your arm through the hole.

STUDENT: (Zeke drops the coat on the floor and begins crying loudly. He continues to cry continuously for over 60 seconds without attempting the task or asking for help.)
