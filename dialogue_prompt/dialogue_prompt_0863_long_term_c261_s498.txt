DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: The game is over. It is time to clean up the toys and put your clothes back on.

STUDENT: (Trudging slowly and wearily) I don't know how to put things away.

TUTOR: Please get dressed first.

STUDENT: (Complaining and taking considerable time to get dressed) I need to use the toilet.
