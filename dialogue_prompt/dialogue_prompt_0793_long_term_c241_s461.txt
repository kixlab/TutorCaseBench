──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: The bell just rang for break time. The other students are taking the ball to the school yard to play soccer.

STUDENT: (Intrudes on the classmates' game and annoys them) Hey, look at me!

TUTOR: Philip, please step back from their game.

STUDENT: (Continues to intrude on the game to seek attention) Give me the ball!

TUTOR: They are playing their game right now.

STUDENT: (Falls on the ground and cries, yelling at his classmates) You didn't invite me to play with you!
