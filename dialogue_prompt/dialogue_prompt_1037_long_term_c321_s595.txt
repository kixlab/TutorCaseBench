SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Julia is sitting at her desk during the morning writing block, tasked with using a graphic organizer to plan and write her first draft independently.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Now that our mini-lesson is over, please work independently to analyze the prompt, use your graphic organizers to plan, and begin writing your first draft.

STUDENT: (Raises hand immediately) I need help. I can't get started on my writing.

TUTOR: Just look at the graphic organizer and try your best to fill in the first section.

STUDENT: But I struggle with the planning part. I have difficulty working independently on this.

TUTOR: Keep trying. Just write down whatever ideas come to your mind first.

STUDENT: (Complaining) I can't! I don't know how to organize it, and my sentence structure and spelling are all wrong. I need you to come over here and talk through my trouble areas with me, or I won't be able to write anything at all!
