DIALOGUE

STUDENT: (David does not sit at the table. He begins jumping and grinding his teeth.)

STUDENT: (David engages in visual self-stimulatory behavior, ignoring the sorting task.)

STUDENT: (David exhibits problematic behaviors to escape the task, scratching at the tutor's hands and kicking the table.)
