SCENARIO

The student is participating in a large group language arts lesson where the class is instructed to read from their workbooks.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Class, please open your reading workbooks to page 20 so we can start our language arts lesson.

STUDENT: (Grabs the workbook from the student sitting next to them) I want to use yours.

TUTOR: Please return your classmate's workbook and open your own.

STUDENT: (Pushes the workbook off the desk onto the floor) No, I don't want to.

TUTOR: Pick up the workbook and turn to page 20.

STUDENT: (Stands up and shouts loudly across the room) Make me! I'm not doing this reading!
