The student is participating in a 1:1 interaction session with a classroom adult to practice requesting more while playing with party string and balloons.

DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Here is the party string and the balloon pump. Press the nozzle to spray the string.

STUDENT: (Tries to press the nozzle but cannot push it down. Hands the can to the tutor.)

STUDENT: (Looks away from the tutor and stares at the wall, ignoring the question.)

TUTOR: Look at me. You need to say 'help' if you want the string.

STUDENT: (Pushes the tutor's hand away and makes a loud grunting noise, turning his back to the table.)
