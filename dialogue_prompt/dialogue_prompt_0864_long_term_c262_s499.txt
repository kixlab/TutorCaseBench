DIALOGUE

STUDENT: (Giving a sympathetic look) I’m so sorry, Lizzie, but only two are allowed in Gymnastics. The space is not big enough for three.

STUDENT: (Doing a headstand) And you’re my friend, who can never be as cool as me… but you have to go out into the lava and get a throne for me to sit on. Go get Joya's small black wicker Teacher Chair.
