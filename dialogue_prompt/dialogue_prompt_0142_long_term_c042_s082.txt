──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Here are the graphic symbols for the music lesson.

STUDENT: (Makes a vocal sound, establishes eye contact, and points to the graphic symbol on the table.)

TUTOR: You are pointing at the door. Let's go outside for a walk.

STUDENT: (Changes facial expression, repeats the vocal sound, and signs her wants by approaching the table with the graphic symbols again.)

TUTOR: We are going outside now. Come with me.

STUDENT: (Withdraws from the tutor, terminates communication, and walks over to a different staff member in the room to convey the message.)
