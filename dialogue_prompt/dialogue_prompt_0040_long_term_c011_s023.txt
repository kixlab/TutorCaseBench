SCENARIO

The student is participating in a group discussion to analyze newspaper clippings and formulate questions.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please look at the newspaper clippings on the table. I want you to collaborate with your group and ask questions based on the materials.

STUDENT: Wait, you want us to ask questions and collaborate? We are used to being told to stay quiet and listen to the teachers.

TUTOR: Yes, please work together to write down your questions.

STUDENT: Why did you select our class for this? Can we propose games that we would like to play and activities we would like to do in class instead?

TUTOR: Let's just focus on the reading task right now.

STUDENT: (Stands up from the U-shaped seating arrangement) If we can't suggest our own ideas, I'm not doing this. I'm going to walk out.
