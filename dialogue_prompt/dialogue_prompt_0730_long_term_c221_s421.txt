SCENARIO

The student is in a seventh-grade science class listening to a lecture about the causes and effects of cancer.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Today we are going to study the causes and effects of cancer. I will be lecturing on abnormal cell division, melanoma, lung cancer, genetic mutations, and viruses. Please sit quietly and take notes.

STUDENT: (Slouches in chair) I don't want to just sit here and listen to a lecture about cell division.

TUTOR: Please open your notebook and start writing down what I have on the board.

STUDENT: (Turns around and starts talking loudly to another classmate instead of looking at the board) This is boring.

TUTOR: You need to stop talking and pay attention to the lesson.

STUDENT: I'm not doing this! Just give me a referral to the office for discipline, I don't care.
