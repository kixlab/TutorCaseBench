DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for independent reading. Please choose a content area text from the science bin and begin reading.

STUDENT: (Spends a large amount of time digging through the bin without selecting anything) I hate reading. Reading is boring.

TUTOR: Keep trying to read the paragraphs.

STUDENT: (Closes the book and stops reading) I'm confused by it. I get to the point that I barely understand it. My interest has gone down in middle school.
