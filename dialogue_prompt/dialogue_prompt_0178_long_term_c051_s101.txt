The teacher is leading a storybook reading activity during circle time to help the student develop language skills.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We are reading the storybook now. What is the animal on this page?

STUDENT: Dog.

TUTOR: That is correct. What is the dog doing in the picture?

STUDENT: Run.

TUTOR: No, that is incorrect. He is jumping. Where is he jumping?

STUDENT: (Looks down, hesitant to use more words) Bed.
