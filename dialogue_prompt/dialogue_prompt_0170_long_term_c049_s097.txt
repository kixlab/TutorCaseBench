DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please open your workbook and start writing the answers for the next section of your qualification coursework assignment.

STUDENT: (Arrives looking upset) I don't want to do this today. Writing is always a massive barrier for me.

TUTOR: Just try to get as much written down as you can.

TUTOR: Since there are mistakes, you need to copy it again on the next page so it's readable.

STUDENT: I'm not copying it again! I have lots of writing to do and it's already full of mistakes. It's just too disheartening to copy it all over again.
