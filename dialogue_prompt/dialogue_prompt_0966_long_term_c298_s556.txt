DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please reduce the fraction 8/12.

TUTOR: Okay, go on to the denominator.

STUDENT: (Looks at the 2, then counts on fingers again) Two, four, six, eight, ten, twelve. That's six.

TUTOR: Write down your final answer.

STUDENT: The reduced fraction is 2/6.
