DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We are going to discuss the Boxcar Children dilemma. The characters have to choose between running away to stay together or going home where they might face severe punishment. What do you think they should do?

STUDENT: If they take away the baby, then . . . they'll take him away. They don't want him to go away. If they go home they might get in trouble. Where he at, where she at?

TUTOR: Should they run away if it is against the law?

STUDENT: Yes. No.

TUTOR: No? Why?

STUDENT: Because it's wrong and [pause]. No. Yes. Yes. I say yes.
