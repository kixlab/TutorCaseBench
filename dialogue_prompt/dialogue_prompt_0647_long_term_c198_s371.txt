DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's look at these shape cards. Point to the blue square.

STUDENT: (Sits passively and does not look at the cards.)

STUDENT: (Fails to respond to the bid for joint attention and remains unengaged, staring blankly away from the table.)
