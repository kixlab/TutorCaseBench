SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is working independently on a reading comprehension worksheet in a noisy classroom and needs to complete the second question.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: I see you skipped the second question on your reading comprehension worksheet. Please go back and complete it.

STUDENT: I went on to do the third question because I saw this was too difficult for me and so that I would not waste time.

TUTOR: You still need to answer it. Try to figure out what the question is asking.

STUDENT: (Sits for a minute facing the text sheet without doing anything) I tried to find out the answer… I didn’t understand the question.

TUTOR: Read the text again and see what you can find to write down.

STUDENT: (Looks at the assignment sheet while holding her head between her hands, reads the text, returns to the assignment sheet, picks up her pencil, puts it down. Sits silently without asking for help.)
