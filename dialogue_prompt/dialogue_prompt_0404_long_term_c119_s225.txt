DIALOGUE

Your job coach says you have a tendency to pay more attention to what others are doing and can lose focus on what you need to be doing. Do you think that working on finishing tasks without stopping so much would be a good goal to work on?

TUTOR: So that is something that you could work on at both school and home. Do you think it is a problem, Michael?

TUTOR: We need to decide on a goal for your transition plan today. Is it okay if we just go with this one?

STUDENT: Sure.
