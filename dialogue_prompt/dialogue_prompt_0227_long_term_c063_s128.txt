──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is sitting in a circle with the class for a philosophical inquiry session and is expected to listen to a stimulus text and wait for their turn to speak.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We are starting our philosophical inquiry session. I will read the text, and then we will take turns sharing our thoughts with the group.

STUDENT: (Raises hand immediately before the text is finished) I want to say something right now!

TUTOR: Please wait your turn, we are still listening to the story.

STUDENT: (Drops hand and turns to the student sitting next to him in the circle) Hey, look at what I can do with my shoes.

TUTOR: Let's try to stay on task and focus on the discussion.

STUDENT: (Interrupts the group discussion loudly) This is taking too long! Watch this, everyone! (Starts making noises and waving his arms to get the attention of the other children in the circle)
