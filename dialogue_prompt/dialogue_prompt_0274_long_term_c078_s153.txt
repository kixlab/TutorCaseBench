Ben is asked to join a whole-class activity in the classroom where a dog doll is present on a nearby shelf.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Ben, please join the rest of the class on the rug so we can start our morning activity.

STUDENT: (Fidgets constantly and refuses to walk toward the rug, looking nervously around the classroom) I don't want to go over there.

TUTOR: Everyone else is sitting down. Come take your spot.

STUDENT: (Points toward the shelf next to the rug, showing visible emotional distress) No, the dog doll is sitting right there.

TUTOR: It's just a toy on the shelf. Please sit down with the class.

STUDENT: (Backs away quickly from the area, crying and exhibiting intense fear of the object, completely unable to participate in the class activity) No! I'm scared of it! I can't sit there!
