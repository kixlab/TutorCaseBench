DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Today we are drawing someone's face. Here is the exact structure you must follow, and we need to get this done quickly.

STUDENT: I can't just start tinkering around with the middle, like the eyes and the nose and the mouth, when there's so much pressure to do it quickly.

TUTOR: Do your best to get it done.

STUDENT: I feel too much pressure. If you structure it like this, I need to have someone hold my hand throughout the entire thing. I need everyone's help!
