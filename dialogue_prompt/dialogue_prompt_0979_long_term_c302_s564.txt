DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We finished jumping. Let's check the picture schedule. It says catch. Please stand on the spot.

TUTOR: (Kneels about 2 feet in front of the student) Hands out, look at the ball.

STUDENT: (Catches the ball with two hands, then squeezes the ball)

TUTOR: Okay, let's do that again.

STUDENT: (Bounces on the ball, makes high pitch noises and flaps hands)
