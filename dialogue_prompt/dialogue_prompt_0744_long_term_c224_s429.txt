SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is participating in a small-group discussion about a cartoon panel showing eagles picking on a small bird to explore the feelings of bullying victims.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's look at this cartoon panel with the eagles picking on the small bird and discuss what is happening.

STUDENT: (Waves hands high) Oh! oh! They are bullying the small bird. I want to talk about this instead of making our own cartoons.

TUTOR: Okay, what do you think about the bullying in the cartoon?

STUDENT: It makes me really upset. I know about the recent deaths of students nationwide because of bullying.

TUTOR: What would you do if you saw someone being bullied like that?

STUDENT: (Facial expression changes to angry) If he saw somebody getting bullied, he would punch the one who started it!
