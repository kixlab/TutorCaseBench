SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Jamal is sitting at his desk in a class of 25 students and is asked to write his numbers on a piece of paper.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Jamal, please sit at your desk and write your numbers.

STUDENT: (Loudly, seeking attention from the rest of the class) I don't want to write numbers!

TUTOR: You need to practice writing them. Go ahead and start on your paper.

STUDENT: (Tears his paper)

TUTOR: Let's get a new piece of paper and try again.

STUDENT: (Falls into a heap and cries) My daddy was killed! (Kicks at his desk)
