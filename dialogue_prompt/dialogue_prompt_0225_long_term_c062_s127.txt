SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student arrives late on the first day of General English class and interacts with the teacher about the seating arrangements.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Welcome to General English. You are arriving late on the first day.

STUDENT: (Walks in quietly and unassumingly with a scowl on his face, speaking quickly in short bursts) Is there going to be a seating chart in the class?

TUTOR: No, there is no seating chart.

STUDENT: Good. (Promptly sits in a seat, slouching down and crossing his arms across his chest)

TUTOR: Now that you are seated, please take out your materials so we can begin the lesson.

STUDENT: I do my work but at the same time I don’t do anything in class. I didn’t really care about anything or anyone.
