DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Let's look at what your teacher wrote here about your interactions in class.

TUTOR: Here is the feedback your mother provided about your behavior at home.

STUDENT: I do not show agreement with either my individual teachers or my individual mothers’ perceptions of my emotional states or my symptoms and behaviour! My emotional intelligence is at a higher level than that of my peer group.
