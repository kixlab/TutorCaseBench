The student is participating in a PE lesson and is instructed to join a team basketball game with their peers.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time to start the team basketball game. Please join your assigned teams on the court.

STUDENT: I'm not playing this. It's a waste of time.

TUTOR: You need to try your best and join the game.

STUDENT: (Steps back from the court) I said I'm not doing it. The teams aren't even fair.

TUTOR: Just go out there and participate with your peers.

STUDENT: (Shoves a peer who walks by) Watch where you're going! I'm out of here, I'm not doing this stupid game!
