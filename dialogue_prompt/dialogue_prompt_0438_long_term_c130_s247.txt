SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is asked to write a paragraph about a story and read it aloud during a standard English lesson in the classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please write a paragraph about the story we just read, and then we will practice reading it out loud.

STUDENT: (Sits very quietly and writes exactly one sentence on the paper.) Done.

TUTOR: You need to write more than one sentence. What else can you add?

STUDENT: (Looks at the paper, using very basic verbal communication) Help me.

TUTOR: Just try your best to keep writing on your own.

STUDENT: (Puts the pencil down and remains very quiet, struggling to be resilient and waiting dependently for the teacher's direct input instead of continuing the writing task.)
