SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is participating in a group play session to build a plastic track for a motor car.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's build a track together using these plastic pieces so the motor car can move.

STUDENT: (Sings in a high-pitched, changed voice) I get to build it! Give me all the pieces right now!

TUTOR: We need to share the pieces to build the track.

STUDENT: No, you're messing it up! It's your fault the car won't go!

TUTOR: Let's just keep trying to put the track together.

STUDENT: (Throws a plastic track piece) You are stupid! I am controlling the car, not you!
