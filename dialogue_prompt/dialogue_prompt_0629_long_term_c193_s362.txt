DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Please open your book and start reading the story from our required curriculum.

STUDENT: I don't have any supplies for the first day of school. Besides, this is boring and I'm worried that I won't be able to focus on reading a book.

STUDENT: (Puts head on the desk) I can't do it.

TUTOR: Please sit up and keep trying to read the story.

STUDENT: (Falls out of their seat, then gets up to sharpen the borrowed pencil for the fourth time in 10 minutes, 'accidentally' hitting another student on the way)
