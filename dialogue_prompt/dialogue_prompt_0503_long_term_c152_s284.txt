──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for our independent creative writing period. Please stay at your desks and work on your stories.

STUDENT: (Fidgets in his chair and quickly scribbles on the paper) I'm done. I skipped some words and made mistakes, but I'm finished.

TUTOR: Please look over your paper and keep trying.

STUDENT: (Stands up beside his desk) I don't want to look it over. Hey guys, look at me!

TUTOR: Alex, you need to be quiet and do your writing.

STUDENT: (Leaves his seat and walks over to distract his peers, making a comical face) Watch this! I just didn't know how to have fun doing this boring writing!
