Christopher is entering the classroom and is expected to sit down and complete his set work.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Christopher, please follow the routine for entering the classroom and sit down to finish your set work.

STUDENT: (Enters the classroom but does not sit still at his desk, showing he cannot meet the expectation of the routine.)

TUTOR: You need to sit still and focus on your paper.

STUDENT: (Turns away from the desk and starts talking to the other children instead of doing the set work.)

TUTOR: I am watching you, Christopher. You need to finish your work right now.

STUDENT: (Reacting to the magnitude of the scrutiny, he stands up, leaves his unfinished set work on the desk, and begins walking around the class.)
