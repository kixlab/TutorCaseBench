Eric is sitting in the classroom working on a Chinese language worksheet to practice word recognition and sentence writing.

DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for your Chinese language class work. Please look at the worksheet and read the first Chinese word out loud to me.

STUDENT: (Moves about in his seat and starts biting his finger) I don't know how to say it.

TUTOR: Just try your best to read the word, and then we will use it to make a sentence.

STUDENT: (Continues biting his finger and moving around anxiously) I can't read it!

TUTOR: You need to focus and finish copying the words onto your worksheet.

STUDENT: (Pulls his hair and screams) No! I can't do it!
