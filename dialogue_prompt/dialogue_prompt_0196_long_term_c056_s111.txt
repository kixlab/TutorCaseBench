DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please look at the data generated by the Knowledge Connections Analyzer on your screen and use it to fill out your prompt sheet about our community's performance.

STUDENT: I can see my deficiencies. For example, reading others' notes, I have read many notes, the percentage is 99% which is pretty high. However, when I look at built-on to others' notes, the percentage is only about 20%, which is pretty low.

TUTOR: What do you think about that?

STUDENT: According to the KCA data, my notes have been read a lot, but built on less. What is worse, no notes are referred to by others. I am not happy with the quality of my notes; it has much room to be improved.

TUTOR: Keep looking at the tool to see what else it shows.

STUDENT: I just see my own numbers. The data tells me that the interaction between the other students and me is not sufficient. I even write notes at home, because I am afraid that my point or idea is not good enough.
