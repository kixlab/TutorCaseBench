SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Chris is sitting at his desk at the beginning of math class with his materials out, and the goal is for him to orient toward the teacher to begin the lesson.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Class is starting. Please make sure you have your pencil, paper, and math book ready, and look up here at the board so we can begin the lesson.

STUDENT: (Sitting in his seat with his pencil, paper, and math book on the desk, but looking down and drawing a picture) Hey guys, check out this drawing of the basketball game.

TUTOR: Chris, we are starting math now. Please stop drawing and pay attention to the lesson.

STUDENT: Math is just not my subject.

TUTOR: You need to listen to the instruction so you can understand the concepts we are covering today.

STUDENT: (Continues to doodle on his paper, making a funny face to the peers next to him instead of orienting toward the teacher) I know, when I listen more I get it more easily, but I need to finish this.
