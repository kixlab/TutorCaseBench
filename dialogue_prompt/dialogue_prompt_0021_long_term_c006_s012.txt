DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: For spelling class today, please place the words from your spelling list in alphabetical order, write them three times each, and make up sentences using the words.

STUDENT: (Looks at the spelling list and begins making noises instead of writing the words in alphabetical order.)

TUTOR: You need to finish making up sentences using the words.

STUDENT: (Exhibits completely off-task behavior, making loud noises, inappropriate gestures, and acting "silly" in the classroom.)
