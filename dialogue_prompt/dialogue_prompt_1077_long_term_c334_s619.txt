DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Class is starting. Please put your play items away and sit quietly with the rest of the children.

STUDENT: (Looks at the large group of children, holds onto a small wooden block, and remains completely quiet.)

STUDENT: (Slowly steps backward away from the teacher, keeping their head down and not saying a word.)

STUDENT: (Retreats further into the corner to hide, crouching down in isolation with an unhappy, fearful expression, completely withdrawing from the interaction.)
