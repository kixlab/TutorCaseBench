──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Joshua is playing with toy trucks in the preschool classroom during free play and is asked to share materials with a classmate.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Joshua, it is free play time, so please share the toy trucks and build a road with your classmate.

STUDENT: (Holds the toy trucks tightly) No, I am the boss of the trucks. I am not sharing.

TUTOR: You need to take turns with the toys.

STUDENT: (Growls) Grrr! I make the rules! He has to be the worker who just watches!

TUTOR: He wants to drive a truck too. How can we play together?

STUDENT: (Yells and throws a toy) No! Do this or I will hurt you!
