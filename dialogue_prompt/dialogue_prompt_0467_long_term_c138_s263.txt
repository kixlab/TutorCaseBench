──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Jessica is sitting at her desk with her completed drawing of a teacher and is asked to write an English label for it to match the other labeled objects in the classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Jessica, your drawing of the teacher is done. Now you need to write a label in English for your picture, just like the yellow signs we have on the desk and bookcase.

STUDENT: (Makes a weak smile and nods quietly.)

TUTOR: What words do you want to put on your label? You can go ahead and start writing.

STUDENT: (Says nothing and looks down at the paper with a depressed and helpless look.)

TUTOR: Do you have an idea for the label? Just try your best to write it in English.

STUDENT: No. (Shakes her head quietly, leaving the label absent on the drawing.)
