SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Michael and his tutor are in a one-on-one meeting selecting a self-monitoring tool and setting goals for his transition to Social Studies.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We need to select a self-monitoring tool for your transitions. You can use a self-monitoring app on your phone or a paper chart.

STUDENT: I am concerned about using my phone during classes. I want to use the paper chart.

TUTOR: Alright, we will use the paper chart. What is something you do right now that makes you late to Social Studies?

STUDENT: Going a way I like to go by the windows or something.

TUTOR: Okay. What should we set as your goal for the self-monitoring intervention?

STUDENT: I want to set a goal of being on time for all of my classes.
