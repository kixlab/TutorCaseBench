SCENARIO

The student is sitting in a circle during a Community of Philosophical Inquiry session and is asked to provide an argument about the stimulus text.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We are going to discuss the stimulus text we just read. Please share your thoughts with the circle.

STUDENT: (Turns to the student sitting next to him) Hey, look at what I have over here. (Attempts to distract the other participant)

TUTOR: Please focus on the discussion. What is your argument about the text?

STUDENT: Sometimes it’s really difficult, you need to follow the story and you need to think about what the person’s saying...

TUTOR: Just go ahead and tell us what you are thinking.

STUDENT: I can't do it! (Becomes visibly angry and frustrated, struggling to organize and convey his thoughts without a clear structure)
