──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's get started on your math homework. Please complete the first problem-solving procedure on the worksheet.

STUDENT: (Digs through backpack) I can't find my math notebook or my pencil.

TUTOR: Look at the problem again and see what you can figure out.

STUDENT: (Pushes the paper away) It's too hard and I keep making mistakes. Math is just not my subject.
