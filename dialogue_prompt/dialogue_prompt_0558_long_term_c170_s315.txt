DIALOGUE

STUDENT: (Remains completely silent and does not attempt to speak or sing while looking at the standard music notation.)

TUTOR: Go ahead and pick up the recorder. Try to cover the holes with your fingers to make the notes.

STUDENT: (Is unable to use his tongue to control articulation on the mouthpiece and shows he does not have the cognitive skills required to decode the standard music notation.)
