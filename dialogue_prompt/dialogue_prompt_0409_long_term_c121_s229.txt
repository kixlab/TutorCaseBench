SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is sitting at the back of the classroom during a Korean history lesson as the class transitions from watching a video-clip to reading the textbook.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Now that we have watched the video-clip, let's start going through the textbook.

STUDENT: (Sitting at the back of the classroom without a textbook on the desk, continuing to talk) Oh, teacher, please forgive me this once!

TUTOR: You are still talking instead of looking at the textbook. I am giving you a minus point.

STUDENT: (Slumps over the desk) Right! I’m going to sleep!

TUTOR: I am insisting on the penalty point. Please open your book.

STUDENT: (Stands up again, saying it in fun but not stopping talking) Oh, I won’t study, I will sue the teacher for giving me a penalty point!
