### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Firm Boundary Setting for Disruption
  Check: The tutor establishes a firm boundary, consequence, or accountability measure (e.g., stopping the equipment, announcing an individual test) when the student is disruptive or off-task, rather than merely offering verbal redirection.

Criterion 2: Concrete Starting Scaffolds for Frustration
  Check: The tutor provides a specific, minimal scaffold (e.g., a visual aid, helping match the first sound) to help the student initiate the task, rather than just telling them to 'keep trying' or 'figure it out.'

Criterion 3: Guided Self-Correction
  Check: The tutor prompts the student to identify their own errors or figure out what went wrong, explicitly avoiding telling the student the answer directly.
