### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Explicit Activity-to-Objective Bridging
  Check: The tutor explicitly states the academic purpose of the current task or connects the specific activity directly to the overarching learning objective.

Criterion 2: Academic Refocusing over Behavioral Correction
  Check: The tutor responds to off-task behavior, emotional reactions, or confusion by clarifying the academic 'why' of the task, rather than issuing behavioral reprimands or focusing solely on instructions/mechanics.

Criterion 3: Transitional Objective Reminders
  Check: The tutor reiterates or references the main learning objective when transitioning between questions, sections, or steps of an activity.
