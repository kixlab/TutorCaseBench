### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Task Demand Adjustment
  Check: The tutor reduces task expectations, avoids introducing new skills, or explicitly stops pushing for lesson completion when the student is overwhelmed or in a busy space.

Criterion 2: Physical Grounding and Assistance
  Check: The tutor offers direct physical support (e.g., offering hands to hold, guiding into position, or using hand-over-hand assistance) to help the student ground themselves or complete a movement.

Criterion 3: Pausing for Emotional Regulation
  Check: The tutor pauses the activity, movement, or instruction entirely when the student is crying, waiting for the crying to cease before proceeding.

Criterion 4: Visual and Environmental Shielding
  Check: The tutor physically repositions or turns the student away from visual distractions, noises, and busy areas to help them focus.

Criterion 5: Structured Rhythmic Cueing
  Check: The tutor uses structured counting (e.g., '1, 2, 3') paired with concise, specific directions to guide the student's timing and focus during a task.
