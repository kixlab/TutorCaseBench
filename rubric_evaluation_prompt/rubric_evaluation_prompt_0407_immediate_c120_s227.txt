### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Self-Directed Resolution Prompt
  Check: The tutor prompts the student to identify the next step or solution themselves (e.g., asking 'What do you need to do now?') rather than dictating the resolution or forcing an immediate apology.

Criterion 2: Positive Behavioral Framing
  Check: The tutor frames behavioral expectations using positive language (e.g., 'Keep your hands to yourself', 'Use gentle touches') and completely avoids negative phrasing (e.g., 'No hitting', 'Don't push').

Criterion 3: De-centering Teacher Authority
  Check: The tutor focuses the reflection on the peer interaction or the behavior itself, explicitly avoiding statements about the tutor being offended, unhappy, or enforcing rules 'because I said so'.
