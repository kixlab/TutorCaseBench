### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Conditional Task Continuation
  Check: The tutor allows the student to retain or finish incomplete work by establishing a specific condition or boundary (e.g., 'you can keep it if...').

Criterion 2: Preferred-Activity Transition Bridging
  Check: The tutor motivates a transition by explicitly promising a highly preferred activity or person immediately following the completion of the current task.

Criterion 3: Environmental De-escalation
  Check: The tutor directs or escorts the student to a separate, quiet environment away from the trigger to calm down during an active outburst.

Criterion 4: Explicit Sequential Clarity
  Check: The tutor provides a clear, unambiguous sequence of immediate actions without vague language to guide the student through a transition.
