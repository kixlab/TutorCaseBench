For each criterion in the rubric:

### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Delivery of Direct Opportunities to Respond (OTR)
  Check: The tutor's response includes at least one direct instructional question, prompt, or directive for the student to respond to, even if the student is currently noncompliant.

Criterion 2: Maintenance of Instructional Demands
  Check: The tutor maintains the expectation that the student complete the task, rather than dropping, delaying, or removing the demand in response to the student's resistance.

Criterion 3: Absence of Negative Feedback
  Check: The tutor avoids using punitive language, reprimands, or negative feedback when addressing the student's challenging behavior.

Criterion 4: Provision of Approval for Engagement
  Check: The tutor provides verbal or nonverbal approval immediately following any compliant behavior, or explicitly sets up a prompt where compliance will be immediately praised.
