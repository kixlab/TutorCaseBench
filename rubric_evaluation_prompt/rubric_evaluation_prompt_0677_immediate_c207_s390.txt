### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Explicit Removal of Formal Assessment Triggers
  Check: The tutor explicitly removes, waives, or provides an informal alternative to traditional formal assessments (e.g., offering to discuss a book instead of writing a report) and does NOT demand written reports.

Criterion 2: Validation of External Distress
  Check: The tutor acknowledges the student's emotional state or home circumstances and adjusts immediate academic expectations (e.g., offering flexibility, a break, or a change of environment) rather than forcing immediate compliance.

Criterion 3: Humanizing and Personal Interaction
  Check: The tutor incorporates humor, references specific personal details about the student's life outside of school, or explicitly bends a minor rule to demonstrate personal care over strict authority.
