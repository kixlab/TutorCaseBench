  Check: The tutor explicitly avoids providing direct explanations or playing 'guess the right answer', instead tossing questions back, expressing uncertainty, or asking the student to evaluate the evidence.

Criterion 3: Amplification of Student Voice
  Check: The tutor uses specific talk moves (e.g., asking the student to 'say more', revoicing their idea, or asking peers to agree/disagree) to elevate the student's contribution into a subject of discussion.
