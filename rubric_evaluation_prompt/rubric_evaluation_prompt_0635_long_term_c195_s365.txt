### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Collaborative daily target agreement
  Check: The plan includes regular collaborative target-setting with Brian before lessons, rather than goals being imposed without his involvement.

  Check: The plan defines observable work or behavior targets and links meeting them to a specific, meaningful reward or activity.

Criterion 3: Explicit mutual contract commitment

Criterion 4: Brian-controlled reinforcement and goal choice
  Check: The plan gives Brian meaningful choice over reinforcers and at least some role in shaping his goals.

Criterion 5: Self-monitoring toward independent regulation
  Check: The plan requires Brian to monitor or record his own progress in a way that supports independent regulation during challenging work.

Criterion 6: Protection against novelty-based reward failure
  Check: The plan includes a way to keep reinforcers personally valuable over time and does not rely solely on generic or fixed school-based rewards.
