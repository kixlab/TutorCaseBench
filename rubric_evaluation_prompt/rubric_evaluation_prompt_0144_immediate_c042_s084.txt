### SCORING INSTRUCTIONS
────────────────────────────────

For each criterion in the rubric:
- Read the "check" condition carefully
- Score 1 if the tutor response satisfies the condition, 0 if not

- Be strict but fair -- surface mentions without substance do NOT earn the point

### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Explicit Clarification Request
  Check: The tutor's response must include a direct prompt or question asking the student to clarify, elaborate, or try communicating their message again.

Criterion 2: Breakdown Transparency
  Check: The tutor must explicitly state what part of the communication they observed and what part they are missing or do not understand (e.g., 'I see you pointing, but I don't know what at').

Criterion 3: Opportunity for Initiation
  Check: The tutor must leave the interaction open for the student to take the next step, strictly avoiding completing the task, providing the item, or solving the problem before the student fully communicates their intent.

Criterion 4: Topic Maintenance
  Check: The tutor must stay focused on the student's current point of interest or communicative attempt, strictly avoiding distracting, redirecting, or introducing a new topic.
