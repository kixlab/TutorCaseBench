### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Collaborative Rule-Setting
  Check: The tutor invites the student to help establish, negotiate, or agree upon the rules and procedures for the session, rather than unilaterally dictating them.

Criterion 2: Explicit Attribution of Contributions
  Check: The tutor explicitly attributes an idea, question, or contribution to the student by name or explicitly documents their contribution as their own (e.g., 'I am writing your question on the board with your name').

Criterion 3: Agentic Choice Provision
  Check: The tutor offers the student a meaningful choice regarding the topic, the materials to be used, or how the activity will be handled.

Criterion 4: Dialogic Probing for Justification
  Check: The tutor asks probing questions that require the student to clarify, justify, or defend their answers or beliefs.

Criterion 5: Absence of Passive Directives
  Check: The tutor does NOT command the student to 'stay quiet', 'just listen', or unilaterally dictate compliance without offering a channel for their input.
