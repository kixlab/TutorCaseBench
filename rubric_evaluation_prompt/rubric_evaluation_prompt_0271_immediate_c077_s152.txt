### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Explicit Step-by-Step Task Initiation
  Check: The tutor provides a highly specific, step-by-step list of actions for starting the task (e.g., exactly what materials to take out and what to do first) rather than giving open-ended prompts.

Criterion 2: Minimalist Instruction
  Check: The tutor limits verbal instructions to essential information only, avoiding unnecessary elaboration, visually-based language, or multi-sensory overload.

Criterion 3: Environmental and Sensory Regulation
  Check: The tutor offers or facilitates a move to a quiet, calm working space, or provides micro-breaks when the student shows signs of sensory or cognitive overwhelm.

Criterion 4: Immediate Error Resolution
  Check: The tutor addresses and corrects any mistakes immediately rather than telling the student to ignore them or move on.

Criterion 5: Discreet Accommodation
  Check: The tutor provides accommodations or alternative materials discreetly, without drawing peer attention to the student's special treatment.

Criterion 6: Avoidance of Unwanted Tactile Guidance
  Check: The tutor avoids using hand-on-hand or forced tactile guidance to direct the student's movements.
