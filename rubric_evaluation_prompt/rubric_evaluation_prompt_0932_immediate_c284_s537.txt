### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Task Translation to Own Words
  Check: The tutor prompts the student to rephrase the task, concept, or prompt into their own words or what they 'think they know.'

Criterion 2: Containment of Affective Derailment
  Check: The tutor explicitly acknowledges the student's strong feelings or opinions while redirecting back to the immediate task and promising a specific future opportunity to discuss it.

Criterion 3: Devil's Advocate Prompting
  Check: The tutor introduces an opposing argument, alternative perspective, or 'devil's advocate' stance to challenge the student's stated viewpoint.

Criterion 4: Contemporary National Connections
  Check: The tutor connects historical events to contemporary national or global events WITHOUT forcing a connection to the student's own local community or personal life.

Criterion 5: Supportive Enforcement of Participation
  Check: The tutor insists that the student take a role or participate in the activity, framing it as an equitable requirement for everyone rather than allowing them to opt out.
