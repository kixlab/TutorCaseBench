### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Bilingual Recasting
  Check: The tutor accepts the student's L1 or gestural response and explicitly provides the L2 English equivalent (recast) without reprimanding the use of L1.

Criterion 2: L1 Conceptual Scaffolding with L2 Terms
  Check: The tutor uses (or permits) L1 to explain abstract concepts or provide daily-life examples, while explicitly embedding or highlighting the L2 technical vocabulary.

Criterion 3: Morphological Breakdown
  Check: The tutor explicitly breaks down or draws attention to the structure of an L2 academic word (e.g., analyzing prefixes, roots, or related base words).

Criterion 4: Scaffolded Question Modification
  Check: When the student struggles to answer, the tutor modifies the question by providing clues, Socratic prompts, or making the topic more salient before simply translating the question into L1.
