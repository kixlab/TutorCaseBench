### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Narrowed Focus and Explicit Summary
  Check: The tutor limits the discussion to 2-3 key strategies at most and explicitly summarizes or visually records the main points.

Criterion 2: Explicit Processing Time
  Check: The tutor explicitly provides 'think time' (e.g., 'take a moment to think before answering') before prompting the student for a response.

Criterion 3: Scaffolded Persistence Without Rescuing
  Check: When the student struggles or goes silent, the tutor uses targeted scaffolding questions to help them articulate their thought process rather than providing the final explanation for them.

Criterion 4: Rephrasing for Mathematical Clarity
  Check: The tutor explicitly rephrases the student's or peers' contributions to clearly state the underlying mathematical rule or logic.
