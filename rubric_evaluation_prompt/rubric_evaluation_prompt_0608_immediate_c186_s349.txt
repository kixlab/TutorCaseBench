### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Provision of Perceived Control
  Check: The tutor's response must offer the student a choice, an alternative way to participate, or a sense of autonomy over how/when they engage, rather than issuing a rigid demand.

Criterion 2: Competence Assurance
  Check: The tutor must ensure the student feels capable of the immediate task by breaking it down, removing competitive pressure, or explicitly validating their ability to succeed before asking them to perform.

Criterion 3: Proactive Environmental Supervision
  Check: The tutor must maintain active supervision, explicitly avoiding turning their back, walking away, or leaving the student alone near physical hazards or tempting equipment.

Criterion 4: Magnification of Relative Success
  Check: The tutor must explicitly identify and praise a specific positive behavior, effort, or choice, no matter how small, rather than solely focusing on correcting negative behavior.
