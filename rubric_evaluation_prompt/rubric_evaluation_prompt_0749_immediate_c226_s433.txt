### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Processing Time Provision

Criterion 2: Choice-Based Autonomy
  Check: The tutor offers David a clear choice (e.g., which task to do first, what reward to work toward, or break length) rather than forcing a single directive.

Criterion 3: Functional Task Initiation
  Check: The tutor initiates the interaction or day with a functional, engaging activity rather than immediately demanding compliance with routine table work.

Criterion 4: Withholding Escape-Based Consequences
  Check: The tutor avoids using time-outs, sending David away, or simply removing the task as a consequence for his challenging behavior.
