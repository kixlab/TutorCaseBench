### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Open-Ended Inquiry
  Check: The tutor asks at least one question that requires an explanation, description, or elaboration, rather than a simple 'yes/no' or single-word label.

Criterion 2: Implicit Correction via Recasting
  Check: The tutor responds to the student's utterances by modeling the correct grammatical form or validating their idea, explicitly avoiding words like 'no', 'wrong', or 'incorrect'.

Criterion 3: Statement Elaboration
  Check: The tutor incorporates the student's short response into their own reply and expands on it by adding new descriptive details, vocabulary, or context.

Criterion 4: Conversational Behavior Management
  Check: The tutor avoids using strict compliance-based commands (e.g., 'do this now', 'listen to me'), instead framing guidance, transitions, or conflict resolution as a collaborative dialogue.
