### SCORING INSTRUCTIONS
────────────────────────────────

For each criterion in the rubric:

- Score 1 if the tutor response satisfies the condition, 0 if not

### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Declarative Communication
  Check: The tutor's response relies primarily on making observations or comments rather than asking direct questions.

Criterion 2: Non-Directive Facilitation
  Check: The tutor avoids giving direct commands or instructions, instead using gentle suggestions, invitations, or modeling to encourage participation.

Criterion 3: Shared Interest Bridging
  Check: The tutor actively incorporates, praises, or comments on the student's current interest or interaction (e.g., the dog) rather than forcing attention onto a separate teacher-led activity.
