### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Decoupling Auditory from Visual Tracking
  Check: The tutor provides information or reads aloud without requiring, prompting, or encouraging the student to visually track the text.

Criterion 2: Strict Task Simplification
  Check: The tutor explicitly limits the immediate workload, breaking tasks into highly manageable, bite-sized pieces rather than presenting a large packet or long text.

Criterion 3: Short-Segment Pacing
  Check: The tutor keeps reading or instructional segments brief and focused, avoiding prolonged focus on tedious details or extended narrative buildup.

Criterion 4: Protection from Peer Judgment
  Check: The tutor accommodates his need for a safe, quiet environment (e.g., not forcing him to read aloud, validating his distraction from noise) rather than telling him to just ignore his peers.
