### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Explicit Information Filtering

  Check: The tutor delivers feedback in a direct, conversational manner that includes explicit positive reinforcement or appreciation before or while addressing areas for improvement.

Criterion 3: Guided Goal Expansion
  Check: The tutor actively prompts or helps the student to identify at least one specific, higher-level learning goal beyond basic task completion or physical execution.

Criterion 4: Capability-Matched Tasking
  Check: The tutor breaks down the immediate task or assigns a specific role that explicitly matches the student's current, limited practical skill level.
