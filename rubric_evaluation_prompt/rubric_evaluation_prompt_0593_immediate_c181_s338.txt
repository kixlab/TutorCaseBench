### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Active Status Prompting
  Check: The tutor explicitly asks the student a direct question about their current progress, understanding, or next step (e.g., asking what they are working on or how a specific question is going).

Criterion 2: Unconditional Validation
  Check: The tutor provides explicit positive reinforcement, praise, or validation for the student's participation or effort, regardless of whether the student's actual answer is correct or complete.

Criterion 3: Step-by-Step Task Deconstruction
  Check: The tutor breaks the assignment down into a single, immediate, concrete step or provides direct guided practice, rather than telling the student to work independently or figure it out themselves.
