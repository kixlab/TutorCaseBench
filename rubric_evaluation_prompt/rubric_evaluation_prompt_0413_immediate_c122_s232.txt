### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Tangible Reinforcement and Break Provision
  Check: The tutor offers a tangible reinforcer (e.g., toy, edible) or explicitly grants/suggests a break when the student shows signs of frustration or task avoidance.

Criterion 2: Alternative Modality Vocabulary Redirection
  Check: The tutor shifts away from the main reading task/interface to explicitly teach the unknown word using an alternative method (e.g., picture matching, sentence construction) before returning to the question.

Criterion 3: Visual Step-by-Step Prompting
  Check: The tutor provides or refers to a visual aid (such as a cue card or numbered list) and directs the student to the specific next step required to answer the question.

Criterion 4: Simplified Instructional Language
  Check: The tutor uses highly simplified, short sentences and accessible language to deliver instructions or explanations.
