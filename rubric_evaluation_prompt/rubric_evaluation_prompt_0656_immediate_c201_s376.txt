### SCORING INSTRUCTIONS
────────────────────────────────

For each criterion in the rubric:
- Read the "check" condition carefully
- Score 1 if the tutor response satisfies the condition, 0 if not

- Be strict but fair -- surface mentions without substance do NOT earn the point

### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Academic Choice Provision
  Check: The tutor offers the student a specific choice between academic tasks, materials, or starting points (e.g., choosing which worksheet to do first or which picture to write about).

Criterion 2: Multi-modal Task Scaffolding
  Check: The tutor provides a visual example, models the expected written response, or explicitly rephrases the prompt to make the task more accessible.

Criterion 3: Sensory and Physical Readiness Support
  Check: The tutor prompts the use of a sensory tool, fidget item, or specific physical support (like an adapted chair) before or during the task.

Criterion 4: Peer-Mediated Engagement
  Check: The tutor encourages a peer to interact with, support, or sit closely with the student to facilitate their engagement in the activity.
