### RUBRIC (variable-length checklist)
────────────────────────────────

Criterion 1: Elimination of Directives and Test Questions
  Check: The tutor's response must completely avoid giving commands, behavioral directives, or asking test questions (e.g., 'What is this?', 'Say X').

Criterion 2: Strategic Control of Motivating Materials
  Check: The tutor must indicate that they are managing, holding, or restricting access to the physical materials rather than giving the student free access to them.

Criterion 3: Unrushed Pacing and Wait Time
  Check: The tutor must explicitly provide wait time, pause, or allow silence to give the student space to process and respond without pressure.

Criterion 4: Following the Student's Attentional Focus
  Check: The tutor must align their response with the student's current interest by commenting on what the student is doing, engaging in parallel play, or acting animated, rather than redirecting them to a new task.
