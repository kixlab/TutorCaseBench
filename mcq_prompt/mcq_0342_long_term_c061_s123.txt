SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Jesus is participating in a sustained silent reading period in his English class.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE

TUTOR: Keep trying to read.

STUDENT: This topic is of little interest to me, and the words are hard to understand.

TUTOR: Just focus on your book.

STUDENT: (Looking over at certain students in the class) I can't. I'm getting easily distracted by them, and that makes reading unappealing.
