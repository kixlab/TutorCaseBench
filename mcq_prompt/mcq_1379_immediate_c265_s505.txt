SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Martin is participating in an independent reading block where the expectation is to read assigned materials quietly.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Martin, it is time for independent reading. Please open your assigned reading materials and work quietly.

STUDENT: (Turns his body completely away from the assigned reading materials and looks around the room)

STUDENT: (Stands up from his desk and walks over to the student sitting next to him) Hey, what are you looking at?

TUTOR: Martin, the expectation right now is to be quiet.

STUDENT: (Remains out of his seat, keeping his eyes and body directed away from his own desk, and continues talking to his peer) I don't want to read, let's talk about recess.
