SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: I am reviewing the online discussion board for your research team as the final project deadline approaches. How is the collaboration going?

STUDENT: I posted a message to the group because most of you thus far have only told me that the [questions] are crap, too long, and irrelevant ...well that is great and all, but it is not too helpful or productive.

TUTOR: What happened after you shared that on the discussion board?

STUDENT: When my teammates responded with apologies and constructive feedback, I posted an apology for my venting. I told them I hope it did not come off as being too harsh or bitchy, I really did not mean to be. I just wanted to name it as frustration rather than anger.

TUTOR: Please continue working with your team to finish the assignment.

STUDENT: With the diversity of personalities and the perception of unequal contributions of peers, my emotions have intensified with frustration and anxiety. I feel my own contributions are inadequate, so I have just disengaged from the project.
