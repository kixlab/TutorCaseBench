SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's look at the qualitative data you collected for your critical ethnography. Please start processing the information to find patterns and make sense of it.

STUDENT: I'm trying, but I'm really surprised at my feelings of frustration and inadequacy right now. I've always found my other honors courses relatively easy, so my confidence is really being tested.

TUTOR: Just keep working on the analysis.
