SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

STUDENT: (Reads slowly) The boy was walking and talking.

TUTOR: Can you formulate a different answer to tell me more?
