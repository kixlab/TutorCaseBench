SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

Michael is participating in an IEP meeting with his teacher and support team to develop his transition goals.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Michael, we would like to develop some goals with you. Your job coach tells me you can do your job well, but that you are not as fast as you could be.
