SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Now that the exam is over, please post your answers for questions 1 and 2, the drawing circuits, to your blog.

TUTOR: Please open your blog on the PC and start drawing the circuits.
