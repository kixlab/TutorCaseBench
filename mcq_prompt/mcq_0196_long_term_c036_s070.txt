──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's use this lunch recess to organize your desk and look at your science experiment assignment.

STUDENT: I'm glad you came to help me organize my desk. The hardest thing in school today was science. I wasn’t sure what steps to do in our experiment and I’m not sure when the extra credit is due, either.

STUDENT: I don’t get good grades because I don’t study outside class. I have too many things to do after school so I don’t always get my homework done.

STUDENT: I'll do it later. (Leaves the science assignment incomplete and does not turn it in)

Question
