followed by a multiple-choice question about the most appropriate intervention.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is using an iPad app and a communication book to make choices for a story in the classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's make a story using the iPad app. Look at the photos on the screen to choose our main character.

STUDENT: (Jane cannot see the screen because of her visual problems.)

STUDENT: (Jane uses her verbal communication book to indicate 'None of the above'.)

Question
