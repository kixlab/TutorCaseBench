SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is using an iPad app in a one-to-one session to create a personal story about a recent achievement.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's use the 'Our Story' app on the iPad to tell a personal story about an achievement. Please start by telling me what happened when you presented to your class.

TUTOR: Okay, you went to the front and read your work. How did you feel when you did that?

STUDENT: (Appears withdrawn and self-contained) I stood there. The teacher listened. Then I sat down.

TUTOR: Try to tell me about your feelings. Were you happy or scared?

STUDENT: (Remains withdrawn, avoiding the question about feelings) I finished reading. Will you keep it? Will it be on the iPad forever?

Question
