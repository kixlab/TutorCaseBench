followed by a multiple-choice question about the most appropriate intervention.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is participating in playtime where another child is currently playing with a ball.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: It is time for playtime. You can go join the other children.

STUDENT: I want to play with the ball that boy is playing with.

TUTOR: He has the ball right now. What do you think you should do?

STUDENT: Well he was doing that as well.

TUTOR: Just go over there and try to get along.

STUDENT: (Hurts the other child to take the ball) If I hurt him, he will stop playing with the ball, then I can use it to start my own game of football.
