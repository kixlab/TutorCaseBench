DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Today we are going to try a new activity to practice using your Braille skills in real life. I have some new folders here, and I want you to read the labels and sort them quickly.

STUDENT: I am happy doing what I am used to do. It’s difficult when you have to think fast and answer fast and do things fast all the time.
