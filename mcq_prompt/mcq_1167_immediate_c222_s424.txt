SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is practicing somersaults and rolls on mats in a tumbling workshop while the teacher divides attention among different groups.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: For this tumbling workshop, please practice your somersaults and rolls on the mats.

STUDENT: (Performs a somersault) Teacher, look! Is it good? I want to be sure I'm doing it right!

TUTOR: Keep practicing. I am going to check on the other group of students now.

STUDENT: (Quickly shoves Ines just when she does the exercise so she falls, and laughs with friends) Watch this, I'm going to do the most spectacular jump!

STUDENT: (Takes a long run before jumping, does the required somersault without getting up afterwards, stays lying down on his back, then lightly slaps Alice who is about to do a roll) Teacher, come here! I need help for the stall turn! See, I'm working!

Question
