SCENARIO

The student is expected to begin a math assignment in a noisy, fast-paced regular classroom.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Alright, it is time to start the math assignment. Look up here at the front to see the problems we are doing today.

STUDENT: (Sits still without taking out materials) The teacher needs to tell me exactly what to take out and what to do, otherwise I don't know where to start.

TUTOR: Just get your math materials out quickly and look at the first section. We need to move fast today.

STUDENT: It's difficult when you have to think fast and answer fast and do things fast all the time. The noise from the other students is making it hard to concentrate.

TUTOR: Try your best to focus on the assignment. Look here at this example, and then look there at the next page.

STUDENT: They tell everyone to 'look here' and 'look there'... but I can't look so I don't understand what they mean. It's bad when they say that.

Question
