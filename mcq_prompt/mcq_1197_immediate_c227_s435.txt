SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is practicing a gymnastics jump task using trampolines and mats in a small group workshop.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Look at the instruction sheet and practice the gymnastics jump using the trampoline and the mat.

STUDENT: (Makes a correct jump and puts up the equipment) Okay, done. (Gives a kick to a mat and chats with friends in the group)

TUTOR: Just wait idly in line until it is your turn to go.

STUDENT: (Doubles up while standing in line, then lies on the mats) This is taking too long. (Scans the environment and begins wandering about the gym in search of an opportunity for change)
