followed by a multiple-choice question about the most appropriate intervention.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is sitting with the teaching assistant to complete a written exam.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Here is the next question on your written exam. Please read it and write your answer.

STUDENT: (Avoids social eye contact and looks away from the exam paper.)

STUDENT: I want that the YouTube channel where I listen to music is always on.

TUTOR: We need to finish the written exam first.
