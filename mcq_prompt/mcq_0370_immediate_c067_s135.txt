SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The class is sitting in a circle for a weekly discussion session about a stimulus text.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: We are starting our whole class discussion on the stimulus text now. Everyone is in the circle, so please share your questions or ideas.

STUDENT: (Sits next to her older buddy, separated from her classmates. She listens attentively to the others speaking but remains completely silent and does not volunteer any ideas.)

TUTOR: Catriona, do you have anything you would like to add to the discussion?

STUDENT: Um... I think... maybe... is it almost time for break?

TUTOR: Let's focus on the text we just read. What is your idea about the reading?

STUDENT: (Moves closer to her buddy and looks down at her desk) No... I don't have any questions. I'm just going to listen.

Question
