SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is answering interview questions about the reasons why classroom rules are important at the beginning of the year.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's talk about our classroom rules. Why do you think it is important to have rules in our class?

TUTOR: What happens when you follow the rules?

STUDENT: Important but I do not know why. These behaviors should not be done, but I do not know why.
