SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE

TUTOR: It is time to start our practice ELA test. Please get ready at your table.

STUDENT: (Silences his voice) Can I move my table to the back of the room, into the corner between the table and the cabinet?
