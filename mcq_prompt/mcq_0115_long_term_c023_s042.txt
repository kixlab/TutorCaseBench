SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student is reading a connected text and completing a vocabulary worksheet during core reading instruction.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Please read the next paragraph of the connected text out loud, and then we will fill out the worksheet for the new words.

TUTOR: Just do your best to read the text.

STUDENT: (Pushes the book away) No, it's stupid. (Starts drawing on the desk instead of reading)

TUTOR: We need to finish reading. Please look back at your book.

STUDENT: (Turns around in the chair, completely off task, and starts poking the student at the next desk) Hey, look at this.
