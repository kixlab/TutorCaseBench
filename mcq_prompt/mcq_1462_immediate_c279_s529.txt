SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

David is working individually with Cuisenaire rods to identify quarters during a Year 5 mathematics lesson.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Here are the red and brown Cuisenaire rods. Please use them to identify quarters.

STUDENT: (Physically placing four red rods against a brown rod) ‘Cause there’s four pieces.

TUTOR: So how do you know it’s a quarter?

TUTOR: What are you checking to see?
