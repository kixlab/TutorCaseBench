SCENARIO

Elia is leading her group's presentation on domestic violence, sharing their critique of the problem and suggestions for remediation.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Elia, it's time for your group's presentation. Please share your critique of the problem of domestic violence and your suggestions for its remediation.

STUDENT: (Speaking forcefully) I don’t understand how this can happen, how you can have such a big problem with domestic violence in today’s society!

TUTOR: What else did your group discuss about the conditions that lead to this?

STUDENT: Personally, I want to be a homemaker, but that’s because I want my kids to turn out o.k., and someone has to be home with them. If you’re both working, it’s just like ....

TUTOR: Can you finish your thought? How does that connect to your critique of the problem?
