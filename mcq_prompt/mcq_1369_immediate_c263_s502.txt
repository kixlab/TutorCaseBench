SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

The student and tutor are in a bookstore looking at the Babysitter Club book series to select books for independent reading.

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

STUDENT: I've never really been interested in those books. They just looked boring to me.

STUDENT: You're really going to get me these books? I'm probably not even going to read them.

TUTOR: Read the back cover and see what it says.

Question
