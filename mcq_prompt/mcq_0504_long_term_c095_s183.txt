SCENARIO
──────────────────────────────────────────────

──────────────────────────────────────────────
DIALOGUE
──────────────────────────────────────────────

TUTOR: Let's review the second draft of your essay and work on correcting the run-on sentences and repeated words.

STUDENT: When you revised my essay I understood all my errors and those were mostly the run-on and repeated sentences. So, I have to work a lot to improve my essay.

TUTOR: What do you think you should do next to fix them?
